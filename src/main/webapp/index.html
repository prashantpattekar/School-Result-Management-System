About Our College

University BDT College of Engineering (UBDTCE), Davanagere, is located in the central part of Karanataka, India. Started in the year 1951, it was the only Government Engineering College in the state. The College was named after the donors Bramhappa Devendrappa Tanvanappanavar (BDT), who donated 1.5 lakh rupees for the construction of buildings.
